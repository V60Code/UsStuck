What is the ruling on fasting for someone who is ill?

Certainly. For someone who is ill, Islam provides a concession (rukhsah) to not fast. However, they are required to make up for it (qadha) on other days after they have recovered.

Source:

"...So whoever among you is ill or on a journey [during them] - then an equal number of days [are to be made up]..."

- QS. Al-Baqarah: 184
